The Problem

A Guidance Gap That's Holding Cambodia Back

Every year, thousands of talented Cambodian students miss out on scholarships, global universities, and fulfilling careers — not for lack of ambition, but for lack of access to the right guidance at the right time.

Breksa exists to close that gap — democratizing world-class academic advising for every student, regardless of where they come from.

🌏

Life-Changing Decisions Without Guidance

Most Cambodian students navigate critical academic and career decisions entirely alone — without a mentor, advisor, or clear roadmap to follow.

💸

Quality Advising Is Out of Reach

Existing advising services are expensive, biased, or geographically inaccessible — leaving rural and low-income students behind.

Vision & Mission

Building Cambodia's Academic Advising Infrastructure

We exist to make world-class academic guidance a right, not a privilege — starting in Cambodia, scaling across Southeast Asia.

Breksa is not just an advising service. We're building the foundation for Cambodia's next generation of global leaders, researchers, and changemakers — one student at a time.

Accessibility: Every student, regardless of geography or income, deserves access to quality guidance. We make it possible through technology and community.

Authenticity: Our mentors are Cambodian scholars who've walked the path. Real experience, real empathy, real results.

Scale: What we build in Cambodia will become the model for academic advising across Southeast Asia — transforming human capital regionally.

Our Story

Built by Students Who Lived the Journey

"Built by Cambodian students who went from rural communities to global education — now helping others do the same."

Somphors Tann grew up in Siem Reap, Cambodia, and navigated the path to global education largely on her own — from Jay Pritzker Academy to Aiglon College in Switzerland, Kenyon College, and ultimately Harvard University.

She founded Breksa – AdvisED Global alongside a community of Khmer scholars who've experienced firsthand how the right guidance at the right moment can change the entire trajectory of a life. Now, they're dedicated to ensuring the next generation of Cambodian students doesn't have to figure it out alone.
